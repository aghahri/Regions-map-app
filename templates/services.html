Professional Liability Insurance

Also known as Errors & Omissions (E&O) insurance, Professional Liability Insurance protects engineering and construction professionals from claims alleging negligence, errors, or omissions in their professional services.

What's Covered:

  • Design errors and omissions
  • Construction defects
  • Professional negligence claims
  • Breach of contract allegations
  • Legal defense costs
  • Settlements and judgments

Who Needs It:

Engineers, architects, surveyors, construction managers, and other professional service providers in the engineering and construction industry.

Coverage Limits:

Coverage limits typically range from $1 million to $10 million, with higher limits available based on project size and risk assessment.

Workers' Compensation Insurance

Required by Texas law for most employers, Workers' Compensation provides coverage for work-related injuries and illnesses, protecting both employees and employers.

What's Covered:

  • Medical expenses for work-related injuries
  • Lost wages during recovery
  • Disability benefits
  • Rehabilitation costs
  • Death benefits
  • Legal protection for employers

Texas Requirements:

While Texas doesn't require most private employers to carry workers' compensation, it's highly recommended for construction and engineering firms due to the high-risk nature of the work.
